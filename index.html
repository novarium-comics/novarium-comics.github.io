Novarium Comics

We are the singularity who forged the co(s)mic nebula

We are a studio based in Indonesia. As newcomers, our passion is forging stories that blast into the vast cosmic space within our universes. At Novarium, our constellation of stars form a resonance of art styles. From the intricate tapestries of the Medieval era to the elaborate web of cosmic intrigue, our 'stars' breathe life into storytelling, characters, and worldbuilding.

Beyond the event horizons, our studio aspires to share our cosmic visions on a global stage. At Novarium, our dreams extend far beyond the boundaries of imagination; they reach for the stars, aiming to illuminate the minds and hearts of an international audience.

Join us as we journey through the boundless expanse of our original IPs, inviting you to explore worlds where nebulae dance, stars whisper secrets, and constellations paint tales of wonder. Together, let's forge an interstellar connection through the art of storytelling.

About Us

Novarium's journey took place during one of the comic conference in 2022. Inspired by the event, a new vision sprouted within our cosmic mindsâ€”a desire to create something fresh, a compilation comic. Thus, our journey began.

Starting from scratch, we founded Novarium's foundation with the Demon Pepito circle, laying the groundwork for our artistic journey. Our first venture was a compilation comic, initially exploring a Medieval theme under the same title.

Witnessing the tremendous enthusiasm from the community for our comics, we decided to keep going, evolving under a different name. It wasn't an end but a supernova; we emerged as Novarium, ready to continue forging our universe.
