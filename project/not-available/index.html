Ignorance is a thin veil that protects us from the vastness of the universe.

The endlessness of the universe is not something we can comprehend; it exists for us to ignore because our existence is too fragile for it. However, how powerless we would be if the universe decided to lift that thin veil and bring its boundlessness before our fragile dot of existence.
